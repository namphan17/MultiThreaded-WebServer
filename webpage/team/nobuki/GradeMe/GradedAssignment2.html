Here are the main points I learned about LinkedIn and networking

  • Most employers look at a possible employee LinkedIn page.
  • Wording matters a lot in your resume and your profile. You should be trying your best to show employers what your skills are. You should give direct information while removing clutter.
  • Networking can be fun. You just need to talk to people.
  • If you help others connect, people will help you connect.
  • On LinkedIn and your resume, you should try to show your most important attributes and experiences first.
  • You can get a lot of jobs if you network well, even if there are more qualified people out there.
